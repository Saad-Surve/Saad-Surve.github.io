News Page



News #1: Family trips post pandemic have seen a comparative increase at Magnifiek Hotels compared to other competitors.


The American Times recently conducted a survey of the amount of trips/vacations which were seen in the post pandemic period in the United States of America. The survey observed a considerable growth of about 10 % in Magnifiek Hotels as compared to the competitors. The expected reason is supposed to be the care and strictness that is followed in Magnifiek Hotels in regards to the COVID-19 guidelines.




News#2: 3 out of 10 chefs in Magnifiek Hotels are Michelin star chefs.


A recent survey conducted by the International Council of Culinary Talents (ICCT) has showed that every 3 in 10 chefs at Magnifiek Hotels is a Michelin star chef making it the hotel with the highest percentage of Michelin star chefs around the globe.




News#3: Magnifiek Hotels signs a deal with Saraj Properties to acquire 15 acres of land in India to expand their business.


Mumbai,India: Magnifiek Hotels one of the leading hotels in the world has recently signed a deal with Saraj Properties, the famous builders of India to acquire 15 acres of land spread around the expanse of the Indian subcontinent. It is expected that around 6 hotels will be established in the metropolitan cities of India.


News#4: By donating 10% of their revenue, Magnifiek Hotels have shown a tremendous act of love.


Magnifiek Hotels are doing a really commendable social contribution by donating 10% of their total gross revenue for the first quarter of 2021. They also have pledged to provide employees significant compensation for the loss incurred during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. The founder Warner Rogerson said in an interview,"We at Magnifiek Hotels believe in caring our family as well as the needy. Fortunately we are at a point where we can donate about 10% of our revenue for the needy."
