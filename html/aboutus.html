Particulars of Magnifiek Hotels:
Our first hotel was established in the year 2003 at Los Angeles. Our each hotel expanses approximately 4 acres of land and consists of around 79 rooms in each hotel. Since 2003, our hotels have now expanded to 58 countries and 103 cities. We have a team of around 1,50,000 employees under the guidance of our two founders and a board of directors.
